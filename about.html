Frequently Asked Questions

Helixar Essentials: Frequently Asked Questions

01
What is Helixar's investment focus?

Helixar invests in early-stage startups across various sectors, emphasizing disruptive technologies, innovative business models and exceptional founding teams.

02
What stages of funding does Helixar support?

We invest in seed to Series A rounds, providing initial and follow-on funding to propel portfolio companies' growth.

03
How do I submit my startup for investment consideration?

Submit your pitch deck and business overview through our website's contact form. Our investment team reviews submissions and contacts selected startups for further discussion.

04
What value-added services does Helixar offer portfolio companies?

Beyond capital, Helixar provides strategic guidance, networking opportunities, operational support and access to our global ecosystem to accelerate portfolio companies' success.
